Frequently Asked PPC Questions

Can we manage and design our own landing pages?

Yes you can. We recommend both parties to do the same, yielding faster results. This will be included in our pricing.

What platforms do you use to build the landing pages?

Depending on your requirements, we use Instapage, Unbounce, ClickFunnels, LeadPages and Web Flow.

Do I own the landing page?

Yes! You retain 100% ownership of your landing page.

Will you also code and develop my landing page?

Yes!

How much do you cost?

Our fee is tailored to your specific requirements.

What happens if the landing page doesn’t convert?

Our team is committed to checking and analyzing the progress of your landing page. Based on this data, we will optimize the landing page as required.

What if I don't like the design?

Once we have drafted the design of your landing page, we will share it with you for your feedback, and will finalize it only after your approval. Our team will ensure that you get what you love.

How can I track your work?

You will continue to be the admin of your accounts; our team will only have access to these accounts. So you can check all the campaign metrics on your dashboard.

Do you integrate with CRM & tracking tools?

Absolutely, we set that up for you.

Will my design work with my Wordpress and other platforms?

Yes, your landing page can be integrated into your website, and will be published like any other page on your site.

Do you work on retainer basis, or one-time projects?

Both!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via PayPal, Xoom and Transferwise
