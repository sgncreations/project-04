Frequently Asked PPC Questions

What kind of PPC campaigns do you build?

We build various kinds of PPC campaigns: search and display network advertising campaigns, Google Shopping Ads, and ads on social platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and more.

Will you do a free PPC audit for me?

Yes. We analyze your accounts and campaign-wise performance, and provide recommendations to increase conversions and leads.

I have 50+ campaigns... Can you manage all of them?

We can! Before we start working, we will discuss your requirement in detail, and provide a monthly plan tailored to your needs.

Is there any minimum budget for Google Ads?

The minimum budget depends on your specific requirement. There are several factors involved in calculating the cost of your Google Ads campaign. An audit of your resources and tools can help determine the overall minimum budget.

Do you offer monthly contracts?

We begin with a 2-month contract. Future contracts are monthly.

How much do you cost?

Our fee is tailored to your specific requirements.

How can I track your work?

You will continue to be the admin of your accounts; our team will only have access to these accounts. So you can check all the campaign metrics on your dashboard.

How do you report campaign updates?

You’ll receive a weekly update from our team, and you can access your dashboard to view the key metrics. We also provide a detailed monthly comparison report.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via PayPal, Transferwise and bank transfers.
