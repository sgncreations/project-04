Frequently Asked PPC Questions

Can you audit my Facebook accounts to see if they need fixing?

Yes, with a high-level audit of your Facebook account we identify issues that need to be fixed, and opportunities to improve your performance.

Can I use Facebook ads or Google Ads?

Google ads and Facebook ads have their own benefits for all types of businesses so you need to take care of the fact that these platforms are used differently. 
You can choose both of them since these networks are not competitive. Both the network can target in varied ways and has diversified ad placements that can get you a greater number of customers. 
Both Facebook and Google are effective platforms to get you more leads and conversions. Facebook is excellent for generating brand awareness. Google Ads can help you make a sale when people will start searching for their queries.

Is there a minimum budget to run Facebook ads?

The minimum budget depends on your specific requirement. There are several factors involved in calculating the cost of your Facebook Ads campaign. An audit of your resources and tools can help determine the overall minimum budget.

How much does your Facebook ads management cost?

Our fee is tailored to your specific requirements.

Does Facebook bill me for my Ad spend? 

Yes, that’s Correct. All you need to do is add your credit card to Facebook account before we can start your campaign.

What do we provide to run Facebook ads?

Through Facebook Business Manager account, we get access to your Facebook business page. In case you don’t know, we provide assistance.

Do you offer monthly contracts?

We begin with a 2-month contract. Future contracts are monthly.

How can I track your work?

You will continue to be the admin of your accounts; our team will only have access to these accounts. So you can check all the campaign metrics on your dashboard.

How do you report campaign updates?

You’ll receive a weekly update from our team, and you can access your dashboard to view the key metrics. We also provide a detailed monthly comparison report.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via PayPal, Transferwise, Xoom and Bank transfers.
